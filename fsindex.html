Who are we?
We are a textile import and export company based in Toronto, Canada.
Since 2004, we have been supplied with only the highest quality textiles from the major Canadian textile wholesalers. With international recognition spanning from Mexico to Brazil and even U.A.E, we plan to continue our steady growth and expand our market to the U.S.A. All of our products are made in Korea with consistently proven Canadian quality. Our main products are printed or basic holographic foil on nylon spandex. With returning customers such as the Canadian Figure Skating Association, Canadian Dance Sports Association, Canadian Cheerleader Association, and many more, our quality speaks for itself. Unlike other companies, we can supply various unique designs and coloured spandex to you at a competitive price
